Sources & Attribution

Our mission is to provide accurate and accessible rugby scores, standings, and statistics. To do this, we rely on data from trusted organizations and official sources.

Data Usage & Permissions

Whenever possible, we seek permission from data providers to use and display their information. If you are a data provider and have questions or concerns about how your data is used, please contact us.

Licensing & Attribution

Some data or content may be subject to licensing terms from the original providers. We make every effort to credit these sources appropriately and respect usage conditions.

Feedback & Corrections

If you notice inaccuracies or would like to suggest additional data sources, please reach out via our contact page.

Transparency matters: This project exists to support grassroots rugby and respect the organizations that make this data available.
