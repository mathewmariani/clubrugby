Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2025

This website values your privacy. We aim to minimize data collection and provide transparency about what is stored and how it's used.

What We Store

We use your browser’s localStorage to remember basic settings and improve your experience. This may include:

  • Theme preferences (light / dark)
  • Selected leagues, filters, or layouts
  • Anonymous usage data (page views, device type) via Google Analytics
What We Don’t Do
  • We do not collect personal data without your knowledge
  • We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking
  • We do not sell or share usage data
Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site — which pages are visited, how long users stay, and what devices are used. This data is anonymized and helps improve the site.

Google may collect data according to its own policies. Learn more here .

Your Control

Because settings are stored locally in your browser, you are in full control. You can clear your browser data at any time to remove saved preferences.

You may also use browser tools or extensions to opt out of Google Analytics tracking.

Transparency promise: If we ever collect identifiable data or introduce new third-party services, this policy will be updated clearly and promptly.
