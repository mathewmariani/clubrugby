Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2025

This website values your privacy. We aim to minimize data collection and provide transparency about what is stored and how it's used.

What We Store

We use your browser’s localStorage to remember basic settings and improve your experience. For example, we may store:

  • Your theme preference (light/dark)
  • Selected leagues, filters, or layout settings
  • Anonymous site usage data (e.g., page views, device type) via Google Analytics

What We Don’t Do

  • We do not collect or transmit personal data without your knowledge.
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or track you across other websites.
  • We do not sell or share any usage data.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site, such as which pages are most visited, how long users stay, and what devices are used. This information is anonymized and helps us improve the website.

Google Analytics may set cookies or collect usage information in accordance with its own privacy policies. You can learn more about how Google uses data here.

Your Control

Because localStorage is stored in your browser, you have full control. You can clear your browser data at any time to remove saved settings.

You can also use browser extensions or settings to opt out of Google Analytics tracking if you prefer not to share anonymous usage data.

If you have any questions about how this site works or handles your data, feel free to reach out.

If we ever begin collecting identifiable data or using additional third-party services, this policy will be updated accordingly.
